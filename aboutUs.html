About us

This project is an evolution of Josh's high school graduation project. The high school graduation project was a text-based game written in Java language. In this project, we added dice animation, a user interface, and a ranking page. Originally, we were planning to include our photos on the About Us page, but we decided to replace our photos with Josh's drawings as we thought they looked cooler. I hope you enjoy this game.
